Our Story

About Us

5 Years of Flavor, Countless Acts of Kindness

Proudly serving New York for half a decade, our restaurant stands as a beacon of taste and compassion. We believe in nourishing not just appetites but also souls. With our health-conscious menu and complimentary meals for the less fortunate, we ensure everyone has access to nutritious food. Fifty percent of our earnings are dedicated to aiding the underprivileged, and through partnerships with 26 orphanages, we provide daily sustenance to those in need. At our heart, we're not just a restaurant; we're a community committed to making a difference, one meal at a time.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Events for Impact

Read More
Savoring Hope

Join us for an evening of culinary excellence and compassion at our "Savoring Hope" charity dinner. Celebrating five years of supporting feeding programs in Africa, guests will enjoy a sumptuous meal prepared by our top chefs, participate in a silent auction featuring unique items, and hear inspiring stories of impact from those directly affected by our efforts. Together, we'll savor hope and continue making a difference. #SavoringHope #FiveYearsOfGiving

Education Empowerment Workshop

Empower the next generation by hosting an education empowerment workshop aimed at providing students from underserved communities with the tools and resources they need to succeed. Through interactive sessions on study skills, career planning, and financial literacy, we aim to inspire and equip students with the knowledge and confidence to pursue their dreams and break the cycle of poverty. Together, let's invest in the future by investing in education.

Food Drive Challenge

Rally your community to participate in a food drive challenge to help alleviate hunger in your area. Set up collection points at local businesses, schools, and community centers, and encourage residents to donate non-perishable food items. Throughout the challenge, track donations and celebrate milestones, culminating in a community-wide event where all collected food will be distributed to local food banks and pantries. Together, let's fight hunger and ensure that no one goes to bed hungry in our community.

Backpack Meals Packing Party

Organize a backpack meals packing party where volunteers assemble nutritious meal kits for children in need. Gather food items such as granola bars, fruit cups, and shelf-stable milk, and pack them into backpacks for easy distribution. These backpacks can be given to students to take home over the weekend or during school breaks when access to meals may be limited. This event provides essential nourishment to children and alleviates hunger in your community.
